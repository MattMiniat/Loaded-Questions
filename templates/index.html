Major US Federal Gun Laws enacted from 1998-2018

▪ 2003 – Tiahrt Amendment – prohibits ATF from releasing data showing where criminals purchased firearms – info only accessible to law enforcement

▪ 2004 – Ten-year federal assault weapon ban enacted by President Clinton lifted

▪ 2005 – Protection of Lawful Commerce in Arms Act – prevent gunmanufacturers from being named in federal or state civil suits bythose who were victims of crimes involving guns made by said companies.

▪ 2008 – District of Columbia v. Heller – challenged aspect of the “well regulated militia” portion of the “collective rights theory” in which states have a right to defend themselves, individual rights to possess firearms unconnected to service in a militia

Handguns ban and trigger lock requirements were lifted as applied to self defense and these laws violate the second amendment
